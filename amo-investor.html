08

VIA SERICA:
The Grand Route

The first living journey from Anatolia to the world.

Via Serica is Anadolu Modern's founding creation world, where cultural memory first becomes a living geography of desire.

This route transcends time and place; an auratic journey that awakens the senses, memory, and the desire for discovery.

The silk memory held in Bursa's cocoons flows toward the deep, silent soil of Mesopotamia, the nomadic intelligence of Central Asia, and the thousand-year layers of ritual and craft across Asia.

Along this ancient line, the road itself becomes an adventurous aura. This great two-thousand-year route is now dedicated to those seeking their own trace.

At its center stands the Modern Traveler: not one who consumes the world, but one who carries the scents, tastes, textures, and invisible stories of the lands they cross.
